# Logo

The cytario logo is the most recognizable element of the brand. Use it consistently and with care.

**Naming convention:** Files with the `tagline` suffix include the brand claim beneath the wordmark. Files without a suffix are the standard compact form. The `mono-` prefix indicates a single-color monochrome variant.

---

## When to use which variant

| Context | Variant | File |
|---------|---------|------|
| Marketing materials, website hero, slide decks | With tagline (light bg) | `cytario-logo-purple-tagline.svg` |
| Marketing on dark backgrounds | With tagline (dark bg) | `cytario-logo-white-tagline.svg` |
| App header, sidebar, co-branding | Standard (light bg) | `cytario-logo-purple.svg` |
| Dark UI, dark backgrounds | Standard (dark bg) | `cytario-logo-white.svg` |
| Branded backgrounds (preferred) | On purple | `cytario-logo-on-purple.svg` |
| Dark bg, minimal color | Monochrome white | `cytario-logo-mono-white.svg` |
| B&W print, regulatory docs | Monochrome black | `cytario-logo-mono-black.svg` |

## Clear space

Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "c" in "cytario." No other graphic elements, text, or edges should encroach on this zone.

## Minimum sizes

| Variant | Print minimum | Screen minimum |
|---------|--------------|----------------|
| With tagline | 30 mm wide | 120 px wide |
| Standard | 12 mm wide | 48 px wide |

Below these sizes, the logo becomes illegible. Use the standard variant at small sizes.

## Brand governance

The cytario mark, logo and visual identity are intellectual property of Slash-m GmbH. The current logo suite was approved on **05.11.2024**.

**Modification requests:** Any proposed change to the logo -- including new color variants, size adaptations, or co-branding lockups -- must be submitted for approval before production. Do not create new logo variants independently.

**Registered trademark:** The cytario name and logo carry the registered trademark symbol (&#174;). Include the &#174; mark on first prominent use in any document or material. It is already incorporated into the approved logo files.

**Regulatory use:** For regulatory submissions (Technical Documentation, Instructions for Use, Declaration of Conformity), use the **monochrome black** variant (`cytario-logo-mono-black.svg`) to ensure legibility in grayscale printing and archival formats.
