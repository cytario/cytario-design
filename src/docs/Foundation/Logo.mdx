## When to use which variant

| Context | Variant | File |
|---------|---------|------|
| Marketing materials, website hero | Full logo | `cytario-logo-full.svg` |
| App header, sidebar, co-branding | Reduced (default) | `cytario-logo-reduced.svg` |
| Branded backgrounds (preferred) | On purple | `cytario-logo-on-purple.svg` |
| Dark UI, dark backgrounds | Reduced inverse | `cytario-logo-reduced-inverse.svg` |
| Dark bg, minimal color | Reduced white | `cytario-logo-reduced-white.svg` |
| B&W print, regulatory docs | Reduced mono | `cytario-logo-reduced-mono.svg` |

## Clear space

Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "c" in "cytario." No other graphic elements, text, or edges should encroach on this zone.

## Minimum sizes

| Variant | Print minimum | Screen minimum |
|---------|--------------|----------------|
| Full logo | 30 mm wide | 120 px wide |
| Reduced logo | 12 mm wide | 48 px wide |

Below these sizes, the logo becomes illegible. Use the reduced variant at small sizes.

## Brand governance

The cytario mark, logo and visual identity are intellectual property of Slash-m GmbH. The current logo suite was approved on **05.11.2024**.

**Modification requests:** Any proposed change to the logo -- including new color variants, size adaptations, or co-branding lockups -- must be submitted for approval before production. Do not create new logo variants independently.

**Registered trademark:** The cytario name and logo carry the registered trademark symbol (&#174;). Include the &#174; mark on first prominent use in any document or material. It is already incorporated into the approved logo files.

**Regulatory use:** For regulatory submissions (Technical Documentation, Instructions for Use, Declaration of Conformity), use the **monochrome** variant (`cytario-logo-reduced-mono.svg`) to ensure legibility in grayscale printing and archival formats.
